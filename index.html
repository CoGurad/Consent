ONLINE CORONAVIRUS RESEARCH INFORMATIONAL TEXT ON PERSONAL DATA PROCESSING

This Informational text has been prepared in accordance with the Numbered 89/2007 Personal Data Protection Law (Article 13) and the Regulation of Procedures and Principles to Be Followed in the Fulfilment of the Information Obligation.

I. Data Controller

In terms of personal data processed in this application, the controller is the Assoc. Prof. Dr. Duygu Çelik Ertuğrul (Eastern Mediterranean University) and Asst. Prof. Dr. Demet Çelik Ulusoy (Eastern Mediterranean University) which is resident at Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus.

In combination with appropriate testing strategies, both symptoms checker and contact tracing functionalities can provide information on the level of virus circulation, and help to assess the impact of physical distancing and confinement measures.

II. Purposes for Processing Personal Data

Personal data may be processed for the following purposes:

Personal data may be processed in order to by evaluating new coronavirus (COVID-19) symptoms.

Personal data may be processed advising person to visit a health facility in case the assessments are positive or high-risk group with a high probability of virus transmission.

Personal data may be managed to make statistical retrieval with the information entered.

The processed data will only be retained for the time period required for these purposes.

Your personal data can be processed to inform you about the measures we establish and the services we suggestion.

If there is any modification in the purpose of processing your personal data, this change will be cleared by updating our informational text and for this purpose your consent will also be obtained for processing your personal data.

III. Processing of your personal data

Your personal data below are processed by us in accordance with and limited to the above-mentioned purposes.

Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

Name, last name, identification number, social security number, citizenship information, mother's name, father's name, birthplace, date of birth, marital status, weight, height.

Communication Data

Address information, phone number, fax, e-mail address, IP address

Sensitive data

Biometric data, daily reported health data (observed symptoms, fever, lung respiratory sound record, throat / tonsil images, general face view video record), presence of pre-existing medical conditions, BMI, previous tests results of SARS-COV-2.

Location

Home address, location, city, country where it is located on continuity or temporarily.

IV. Transfer of personal data

Your personal data processed in this application is not transferred to third parties (individuals, institutions, and organizations) for any purpose. Personal data collected is shared with the TRNC Ministry of Health in accordance with the conditions stipulated in the Personal Data Protection Law in accordance with the objectives set out above.

V. Personal Data Collection Method and Legal Reason

Your personal data are collected in fully automated ways through the application on this program. Your personal data are processed in accordance with Law (Art. 6) on Personal Data Protection issue to the consent of the subject and on the legal grounds stated in the second paragraph of Article 7 of the Law and shared only with the institution specified in the text.

VI. Ensuring the Security and Confidentiality of Personal Data

The opportunity to access and right to ask for change is always given to the user about his/her own data in the program. In accordance with Article 12 of the Law on PDP, the program includes the necessary technical measures to prevent unintentional or illegal harm loss, modification, and transmission to unauthorized persons. It also takes proper institutional and technical measures to ensure its confidentiality and protection from exposure to any other illegal process.

The measures taken in this context are;

  • Support is obtained from experts in technical matters. In this framework the necessary software and infrastructure is available to ensure security.
  • Technical measures are in place to protect the data and software including virus protection systems are used.
  • Access to the data being processed by our program is limited and access to data is possible by authorized persons.
  • A backup program is used to ensure that the personal data is stored securely.
  • In order to ensure the confidentiality of personal data those who are in the position of data processing and / or access to data have responsibilities under the confidentiality obligation.
  • If personal data is shared with the institution expressed in this text, the data transmitted will have an obligation to ensure the data security and confidentiality of the relevant party.
  • Since personal data is evaluated by joint/us-affiliated medical professionals, it is also under the obligation of confidentiality under the Laws of TRNC within the framework of patient-medical professional privacy.

VI. Deletion, Destruction and Anonymization of Personal Data

Storing of information on the user’s device or gaining access to the information already stored is allowed only if the user has given consent.

Personal data will be stored for as long as it is necessary to fulfil the above-mentioned objectives. if the reasons that require processing are eliminated, although it is processed personal data is deleted or anonymized by us at the request of the personal data holder in accordance with the provisions of the relevant Law No. 89/2007 on Protection of Personal Data.

VIII. Rights of the Persons concerned

You can use your personal data access and objection rights stipulated in Articles 14, 15, and 16 of the Law on Protection of Personal Data No 89/2007 by applying to our communication link through Program.

IX. Application to the Controller

You can submit your applications for your access and objection rights in writing or electronically at this duygu.celik@emu.edu.tr address.
